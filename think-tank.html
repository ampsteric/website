What is Think Tank 1.0?


Think Tank is an event, hosted by Farzi Engineer, is a design sprint-like event; often, in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, domain experts get together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project.


We have 4 tanks for all you guys!


  • 1. Game Tank : This tank is for all the gaming geeks! Do you think you have an idea that can just burst the gaming community? Well present it here and reach your dreams superfast!

  • 2. Social Tank : Nowadays Social Media Platforms are booming and there are many sorts of social media platforms out there. If you think you have an idea related to social media platforms, this tank is for you! Present your ideas today!

  • 3. Knowledge Tank : Convenience is one factor that is widely loved by the audience. Any idea that will be providing knowledge such as news headlines with convenience will excel. Submit your ideas now!

  • 4. Tool Tank : Digital Media Tools provide a lot of ease to people while working. It’s so hard to find that perfect tool that’ll work for you. If you have an idea that you’re confident everyone would like, we’re here to listen!

All the students are invited to participate in this challenge as per the following Details:


Team Size: 2-4
Challenge Timelines:


  • - Idea Submission

    This is the first step where you’ll share your idea in a proper format that we’ll be providing. The time limit for this idea would be 1 week. Make this submission so enticing that we couldn’t stop us moving you to the next round!

  • - POC and Validation

    10 teams would be selected after the first round and then you’ll move ahead for POC & Validation. The time limit for this step is 2 weeks. Make your every step count!

  • - Final Prototype with Revenue Generation

    3 teams would be selected in this round where you’ll present the final prototype and show the revenue generation for the idea proposed and marketing techniques that you’ll be adopting.

Rewards:

Once the top 3 teams clear all the rounds successfully, we’ll be providing funding to them to make that product perfect and out in the market.
