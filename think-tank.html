  • Idea Submission

    This is the first step where you’ll share your idea in a proper format that we’ll be providing. The time limit for this idea would be 1 week. Make this submission so enticing that we couldn’t stop us moving you to the next round!

  • POC and Validation

    10 teams would be selected after the first round and then you’ll move ahead for POC & Validation. The time limit for this step is 2 weeks. Make your every step count!

  • Final Prototype with Revenue Generation

    3 teams would be selected in this round where you’ll present the final prototype and show the revenue generation for the idea proposed and marketing techniques that you’ll be adopting.

  • Rewards

    Once the top 3 teams clear all the rounds successfully, we’ll be providing funding to them to make that product perfect and out in the market.
